# Privacy Policy for Screen Recorder Extension

**Last Updated:** December 9, 2024

## Overview

Screen Recorder ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles data.

## Data Collection

**We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The Extension:
- ✅ Does NOT collect user information
- ✅ Does NOT track user behavior
- ✅ Does NOT store recordings on external servers
- ✅ Does NOT use analytics or tracking services
- ✅ Does NOT share data with third parties

## How the Extension Works

1. **Screen Recording:** Uses Chrome's built-in `desktopCapture` API to capture your screen
2. **Audio Recording:** Uses `getUserMedia` API to access your microphone (with permission)
3. **Local Processing:** All recording happens locally in your browser
4. **Local Storage:** Videos are saved directly to your Downloads folder
5. **No Upload:** No data is ever uploaded to external servers

## Permissions Explained

The Extension requests the following permissions:

### desktopCapture
- **Purpose:** To capture screen, window, or tab content
- **Usage:** Only activated when you click "REC"
- **Data:** Processed locally, never transmitted

### downloads
- **Purpose:** To save recorded videos to your Downloads folder
- **Usage:** Triggered automatically after recording stops
- **Data:** Saved locally on your device only

### Microphone (optional)
- **Purpose:** To record audio commentary
- **Usage:** Only when you grant permission
- **Data:** Mixed with video locally, never transmitted

## Data Storage

- **Recordings:** Saved only to your local Downloads folder
- **Settings:** No settings are stored
- **History:** No recording history is kept
- **Cookies:** The Extension does not use cookies

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does NOT use any third-party services, including:
- Analytics platforms
- Crash reporting tools
- Advertising networks
- Cloud storage services

## User Rights

You have complete control:
- ✅ Grant or deny microphone permission anytime
- ✅ Delete recordings from your Downloads folder
- ✅ Uninstall the Extension anytime
- ✅ No account or registration required

## Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.
